Swipe to Soulmate: What Drives Real Connection

Exploring the impact of intent and experience on dating app success

Hypothesis

Users who express clear intent in their profiles and receive highly rated app experiences are more likely to report long-term dating success.

Context

In a world where dating apps have become a primary way people connect, I was curious about what actually drives success beyond the swipe. Are matches random, or are there underlying patterns that shape deeper outcomes? I chose this topic to explore how intent, user behavior, and app experience intersect to influence real romantic connections. With dating app usage at an all-time high‚Äîespecially among Gen Z‚ÄîI wanted to unpack whether people who use these platforms with purpose and consistency are more likely to find lasting results.
